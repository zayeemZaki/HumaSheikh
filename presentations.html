Conference Presentations

October 2024

“My Father and His Khan Dress”

53rd Annual Conference on South Asia, University of Wisconsin–Madison

The paper explores personal and political memory through the lens of family, particularly her father’s murder, and examines themes of gender and cultural identity in the context of conflict in Kashmir. Engaging with ideas of loss, inheritance, and resistance, the presentation contributed to a broader conversation on diasporic narratives and postcolonial belonging.
